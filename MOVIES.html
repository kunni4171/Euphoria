Haikyu

Haikyuu means Volleyball in Japanese and thaes what the series is about. The best thing about Haikyuu is the individual character development of all the characters and the occasional funny jabs make it even ore fun. So intense, so amazing. One of my favorites! Haruichi Furudate wrote the series as a gritty tale of passion and love for the sport and a dialogue from an episode goes like, "Dont you dare look down. Hold your heads high, because Volleyball is a sport where we are always looking up."
