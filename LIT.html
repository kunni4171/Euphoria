Dear Bunny and Aditi,

Friendships are fragile. Aren't they? Things change and friends leave. But you would always find that same friend yelling at the top of his lungs, shouting, "I am still here you idiot, right behind your back. You gotta do this and leap of faith. Don't be a coward." And probably this is that one friend you laugh with. You play with them, you shout at them or you might even share a love-hate relationship with them. You annoy them with your silliest pranks, but they will listen to all your rants at any odd hour of the day, they make you see the sun at the place where you once saw the cloud, and they will always be your ray of hope and light in this compact fragile shell called 'life'. And that's how it's supposed to be, messed up at times though strong enough to withstand any hurdle that comes our way. So, were both of you, Bunny and Aditi.
