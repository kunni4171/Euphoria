What's your daily routine in college?

Getting up and running to classes in 5 min, then sleeping in every class, then roaming in campus till the last minute of intime, then sitting in common room god knows what we did till 3am finally binge-watching some web series and then sleeping till 9am. Basic routine of an engineering student.
